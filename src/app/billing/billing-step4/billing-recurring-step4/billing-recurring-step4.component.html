PullPayment Accounts and Gas Costs

Every time a new customer subscribes to a billing model, the system creates a new PullPayment account. The PullPayment account will continuously bill the customer according to the billing model’s parameters. The PMA tokens will be kept in the PullPayment accounts until they are transferred to the Treasury.

Choose how often you want to transfer funds from all the PullPayment accounts associated with this billing model to your Treasury. Depending on your choice, the specific amount of ETH is allocated for those recurring payments to pay for the gas fees of the transfer to the Treasury.
